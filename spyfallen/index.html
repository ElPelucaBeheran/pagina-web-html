Setup the game

Spyfall is a game where all players receive the same concept, except one or more who are spies and don't know it. Taking turns, each player says a word or idea related to the concept, without naming it directly. The spies listen and try to guess the concept, saying something that doesn't expose them. At the end of each round, players decide whether to accuse someone of being a spy, and the spies can attempt to say what they think the concept is if they are chosen.
